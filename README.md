## 📌 Project Overview
 
This project is an **interactive Power BI dashboard** built to analyze **Global Retail Sales data** across multiple regions, product categories, and time periods. It helps retail businesses understand sales performance, identify top-selling products, and plan strategies based on data-driven insights.
 
---
 
## 🎯 Objectives
 
- Analyze **region-wise sales performance** across 5+ global markets
- Identify **top 10 best-selling products** and categories
- Track **seasonal demand trends** and sales fluctuations
- Enable dynamic **MoM, YoY, QoQ** period comparisons
- Monitor **profit margins** across regions and product lines
---

| **Power Query** | Data transformation & ETL process |
 
---
 
## 📈 Key Features
 
- ✅ **Region-wise Sales Map** — Visual global sales distribution
- ✅ **Top 10 Products** — Best performing products by revenue
- ✅ **Seasonal Trend Analysis** — Monthly & quarterly demand patterns
- ✅ **Dynamic Period Comparison** — MoM, YoY, QoQ using DAX time intelligence
- ✅ **Category Performance** — Sales breakdown by product category
- ✅ **Profit Margin Tracking** — Region and product-wise margin analysis
---
 
## 💡 Key Insights Derived
 
- Identified **top 3 regions** contributing to 70%+ of total global revenue
- Found **peak sales seasons** helping optimize inventory planning
- Analyzed **underperforming regions** with actionable improvement suggestions
- Product category analysis revealed **highest margin items** for strategic focus
